Hole Difficulty Analysis 2025

Based on gross scores from all tournaments this year at Sterling Grove Golf and Country Club.

Overview

This page analyzes the difficulty of each hole based on average gross scores relative to par across all players and tournaments in 2025 (data up to September 13, 2025). The relative score indicates how much over/under par the hole plays on average—higher values mean tougher holes.

Key insights:

  • Hardest Hole: #8 (+1.06 over par) – A challenging par 4 that often sees higher scores due to its length and hazards.
  • Second Hardest: #5 & #12 (+0.99) – Tough par 4s with demanding approaches.
  • Easiest Hole: #16 (+0.32) – A shorter par 4 where players frequently score closer to par.
  • Overall, the course plays about +12.13 over par on average per round based on this data.
