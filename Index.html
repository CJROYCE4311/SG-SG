Latest Announcement

Changes from last year:

Will continue to use Squabbit for sign up and live scoring. We are considering migration to Golf Genius in the future - Stay tuned.

2 man game now has a rotating 3 month of games: Team Best Ball, Team Total Quota, and Team Total Stableford.

Handicaps: 2 Man Best Ball will be at 85% of handicaps per USGA guidelines. Quota and Stableford formats will remain at 100%.

Added CTP for every round.

Lights on the golf course, no matter where (long grass, rock, dirt, etc.), will be played as a free drop for stance and swing, not line of sight.
