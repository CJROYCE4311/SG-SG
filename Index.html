Welcome to the SG@SG League!

Welcome to the Saturday Game at Sterling Grove (SG@SG), a monthly men’s tournament created by members, for members, with the goal of building camaraderie, competition, and fun at our beautiful home course.

What started as a small group has grown into a league of nearly 80 players with monthly events that now fill the tee sheet. Thanks to support from the Sterling Grove Golf & Country Club, we’ve secured an 8:30 AM shotgun start for each tournament so we can all begin and end together, fostering post-round socializing and lunch at Copper + Rye.

Here’s what’s new this season:

New Formats: In addition to our classic 2-Man Best Ball, we’re introducing Quota and Stableford formats (using both team members’ scores). Each format will be played once per quarter.

Handicap Adjustments: 85% of course handicaps for 2-man best ball per USGA standards.

Game Options: Every player enters the main event ($20 buy-in), with optional side games like Net/Gross Skins, Medal Play, and Closest to the Pin — all selected during sign-up in Squabbit / Golf Genius.

Rule Clarification — Lights on the Course: Any ball that lands near lights on the course (including long grass, rocks, or dirt) gets a free drop for stance and swing. This free drop does not apply to line of sight.

You’ll find everything you need on this site: sign-up links, results, announcements, dashboards, and documents all in one place.

We’re excited to kick off another great season. If you’re a Sterling Grove golf member, you’re invited to join us.

Let’s keep it fun, fair, and full of birdies. See you on the course!
